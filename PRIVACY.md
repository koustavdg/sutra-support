# Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026

Sutra is built to respect your privacy. In short: we don't collect your data,
we don't track you, and we don't run analytics.

## What We Collect

Nothing. Sutra has no backend account system and no analytics. We do not
collect, transmit, or sell personal information.

## What Stays on Your Device

- **Library & reading data** — your connected servers, favorites, collections,
  bookmarks, and reading progress are stored locally on your device.
- **Server credentials** — usernames, passwords, and API keys you enter are
  stored securely in the iOS Keychain and are sent only to the server you
  connect to, to authenticate your requests.
- **Downloaded books** — stored on your device for offline reading.

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud progress sync, your reading position is synced through
**your own** Apple iCloud account so you can resume across your devices. This
data lives in your iCloud account; we have no access to it. You can turn this
off in Settings.

## Third-Party Servers

When you connect to a server or catalog (e.g., your Kavita/Calibre server, or
Project Gutenberg), the App communicates directly with it. Those services
have their own privacy practices, which we do not control.

## Children

Sutra is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
information from them.

## Changes

We may update this Policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new
effective date.
