# Sutra — Support

This is the public support tracker for **Sutra**, an iOS reading app for your
self-hosted OPDS libraries (Kavita, Calibre, OPDS) and Project Gutenberg.

The app's source lives in a private repo; this repo exists so you can **report
bugs** and **request features** in the open.

## Report a bug or request a feature

- 🐛 **Bug:** [open a bug report](https://github.com/koustavdg/sutra-support/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml)
- ✨ **Idea:** [request a feature](https://github.com/koustavdg/sutra-support/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml)

Before filing, a quick search of [existing issues](https://github.com/koustavdg/sutra-support/issues)
might find your topic already in progress.

When reporting a bug, please include your **Sutra version** (Settings → About,
e.g. `1.0.0 (1)`) and your **device + iOS version**.

## Privacy

Sutra keeps your data on your device and your own servers — no accounts, no
tracking, no analytics. Please don't post server URLs, credentials, or other
private details in issues.
